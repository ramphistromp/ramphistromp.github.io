About Us

Ramphis Tromp
MBA,  AA,  CICRA,  CICA  &  LI 

Ramphis E.M. Tromp is a seasoned financial professional with over 19 years of experience in accountancy, advisory services, and internal auditing across diverse industries in Aruba and the Dutch Caribbean. Combining technical precision with strategic insight, Ramphis delivers tailored solutions that drive sustainable growth and operational excellence.

He holds a Master of Business Administration (MBA) from the University of Aruba and the prestigious Accountant-Administratieconsulent (AA) designation from the "Koninklijke Nederlandse Beroepsorganisatie van Accountants." He also holds certifications in Internal Control Risk Analysis (ISO 31000), Internal Auditing, Information Security (ISO 27001), and Corporate Governance, among others.

Ramphis began his external auditing and accountancy career, advancing through esteemed firms (Big 4). Over time, he has built and led internal audit departments. His practical, hands-on approach ensures clients receive actionable insights aligned with their unique challenges.
